About the Studio

Wing of Shield Studios is an independent game development studio built on bold ideas and relentless craftsmanship. We develop across platforms — mobile, PC, and web — with a focus on tight mechanics, memorable worlds, and player-first design.

Every game we ship is forged under pressure into something that lasts.
